Frequently Asked Questions

Who owns SpookyMC?

Spooky Kipper

What versions does SpookyMC Support?

Java: 1.8.9 - 1.18.2

Bedrock: 1.18.0 - 1.18.31

Why there are missing chunks on 1.8.9?

Don't be worry, this is a common client-side issue for 1.8.9. You can fix it by reloading the chunk by pressing F3 + A key on your keyboard at the same time.

Why does it says Unknown Command to me?

It will return an unknown command error if the command does not exist or you don't have permission. However, if you think it is an error, you may contact us on Discord.

How can I apply for staff?

You can apply for Helper here: https://www.spookymc.ml/helper-app/

Where can I report a hacker/exploiter?

You should immediately DM us on Discord and record the hacking process for proof.

Where can I buy ranks?

You can buy ranks and perks on our store: https://store.spookymc.ml/

I bought an item from the store but did not receive it what do I do?

If you didn't recieve your item after successfully purchasing from our store, please contact us on Discord with your Username and items you purchased.
